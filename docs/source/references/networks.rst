Networks
========================================
As of now, the following 6 networks are available: 

1. U.S. Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPANET)
2. Cooperación Latino Americana de Redes Avanzadas (RedClara)
3. Italian Network (NSFNET)
4. U.K. Joint Academic Network (JANET)
5. U.S. National Science Foundation Network (NSFNET)
6. Rede (Brasileira) Nacional de Pesquisa (Rede Ipê / RNP)
